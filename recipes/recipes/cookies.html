Cookies

How to make yummy cookies!

For this recipe I'm going to show you how to make the easiest 30 minute cookie recipe.

Ingredients

  • 120g butter, softened
  • 1 medium egg
  • chocolate chips, as many as your heart desires!
  • 75 golden caster sugar
  • 75 brown caster sugar
  • 1 tps vanilla extract
  • 180g plain flour
  • 1/2 tbs bicarbonate of soda

Steps

  1. Pre-heat your oven to 180/160C and line a tray with 2 sheets of parchment.

  2. Mix the butter and both sugars until the mixure is very light and fluffy, then beat in the egg and vanilla extract.

  3. Once all of this is combined, stir in the flour, bicarb of soda and the chocolate chip.

  4. Scoop 10 large tbsp of the mixture onto the trays, leave enough space between each to allow for spreading.

  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until a golden brown.

  6. Leave to cool on the tray for a few minutes and there you have it nice, easy deliciouscookies ready to serve.
