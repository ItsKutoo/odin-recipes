Pizza

How to make a traditional Italian Pizza

For this recipe I'm going to show you how to make the most simple, traditional and most tasty Italian pizza out there.

Ingredients

  • 10 tomatoes
  • Mozerella cheese
  • Tomato puree
  • Pepperoni
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Pre-heat your oven to 200C

  2. First you get your flour, eggs, butter and milk you proceed to mix these ingredients together into a bowl and let set to the side.

  3. After that, you crush up your 10 tomatoes in a food processer or a blender if you don't have a processer.

  4. Get your dough that was setting and flatten it out 8x8 with a rolling pin.

  5. Mixed your tomato puree into the crush tomatoes and spread this sauce on the flattened out dough you made.

  6. Get your mozzerella cheese and sprinkle it out all around the pizza base on the tomato sauce.

  7. Finally, Put it in the oven to bake for 12 minutes, after that you have a Delicious Italtian pizza!
